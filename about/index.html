About Me

I have been dancing since I was 3 years old and in college, I am involved in various dance groups and companies, performing contemporary and modern dance. I also have spent countless hours volunteering with various organizations, including Daybreak Day Camp, Project Friendship, Letters to a Pre-scientist, Food Recovery Network, Prison Book Program, and the Hope Center.

In my free time, I enjoy hanging out in nature, cooking, knitting, and most recently, crochetting sea creatures. I have a pet dog, a Siberian Husky named Bruin.
