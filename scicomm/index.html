SciComm (Science Communication)

What's Cooking

As part of my involvement with Daybreak Day Camp, I helped develop the What's Cooking program, a virtual STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, Math)cooking program for middle school students. The program was three days during school vacation week, run over Zoom with students in their own families' kitchens. Drawing inspiration from the Young Chef's program at Carleton College, I some science/cooking lessons including yeast/baking bread, acid-base chemistry/making pickles, and the scientific method/designing cookie recipes. The program was a result of a collaboration with Cambridge Camping, Lesley University, and financial support from Biogen.
